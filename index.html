02 / READING + UOE

Seven parts.
One system.

Tap a part for the exact strategy.

35 min UoE 40 min Reading
1
Multiple-choice cloze8 questions · vocabulary
＋

Read the whole sentence first. Predict the missing word, then compare the options. Look for collocations and phrasal verbs—not just meaning.

make progresstake a riskdepend on
2
Open cloze8 questions · grammar
＋

One gap, one word. Decide what kind of word is missing: article, preposition, auxiliary, pronoun or linker. Read both sides of the gap.

3
Word formation8 questions · word families
＋

First identify the part of speech. Then check whether the meaning is negative, singular/plural and whether spelling changes.

DECIDE → decision · RESPONSIBLE → irresponsible
4
Key word transformation6 questions · 2–5 words
＋

Never change the key word. Keep the meaning identical and count contractions as two words. Partial answers can still earn one mark.

5
Multiple choice6 questions · detailed reading
＋

Read the questions first. Underline the key idea, find the matching paragraph and eliminate options contradicted by the text.

6
Gapped text6 questions · cohesion
＋

Track pronouns, repeated ideas, time order and contrast words. The sentence must connect logically to both the text before and after the gap.

7
Multiple matching10 questions · scanning
＋

Start with the questions, then scan for paraphrases—not identical words. One paragraph may answer several questions.
